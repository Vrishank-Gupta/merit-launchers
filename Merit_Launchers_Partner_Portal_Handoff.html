Overview

What We Built & Why

A complete partner relationship management system, custom-built for Merit Launchers

The Merit Launchers Partner Portal is a full-stack web system that lets the marketing team recruit, manage, track, and pay a network of associates (Campus Ambassadors, Education Associates, Institutional Partners) — all from a single dashboard.

It replaces manual spreadsheets, WhatsApp updates, and guesswork. Every click, every referral, every commission is recorded automatically.

The Two Sides of the System

Portal Who Uses It URL What It Does
Marketing Admin Portal Internal ML team meritlaunchers.com/marketing-admin Full control — create partners, set rates, process payouts, upload materials, view the full network
Partner Portal Associates / Partners meritlaunchers.com/partner/login Personal dashboard — see their own referrals, earnings, commission history, leaderboard rank, and manage their sub-network

The Four People in This System

🛡️
Marketing Admin
Internal ML team. Controls everything — partners, rates, payouts, materials.
🤝
Partner / Associate
Refers students, onboards new partners, earns commission. Tracks via their portal.
📝
Prospective Partner
Fills the self-registration form. Gets activated by admin or their upline partner.
🎓
Student
Clicks a referral link, downloads the app, buys a course. Drives all commission.

What the System Tracks Automatically

Data PointHow It's Captured
Referral link clicksEvery click on a /ref/CODE link is logged with channel, date, and anonymised IP. One unique click per person per day.
Student referral attributionWhen a student signs up or logs in via the app, their referral code is checked and they're permanently linked to that partner.
Course purchasesEvery purchase in the app is recorded against the student, and the student is linked to a partner — so commission is auto-calculated.
Monthly commissionAdmin generates payouts once a month. The system calculates each partner's revenue × their rate for that period.
Network hierarchyEvery time a partner onboards a new partner via their join link, the relationship is recorded and shown in the tree view.

Commission Rates, Payouts & Toolkit

Managing money and marketing materials

3 · Commission Rates

Go to Commission Rates in the sidebar. You'll see one rate field per partner type:

Partner TypeWhat It Means
Campus AmbassadorTypically students/graduates promoting at their college
Education AssociateTeachers, tutors, coaching professionals
Institutional PartnerCoaching institutes, schools, NGOs

Enter the commission percentage for each type and click Save Rates. This rate is applied to all partners of that type when payouts are next generated. Individual partners can have custom overrides set on their own edit page.

⚠️ Rate changes take effect on the next payout generation

Changing a rate does not retroactively recalculate past payouts. Only future monthly payouts will use the new rate.

4 · Pending Applications

When an existing partner shares their onboarding link (/join/CODE) and someone fills the form, the application lands here. The admin sees the full queue across the entire network.

ℹ️ Who actually approves?

The referring partner (the one who shared the join link) approves applicants from their own portal's Network page. The admin's Pending page is a monitoring view — you can see who's waiting, but approval is done by the partner. The admin can also approve directly from the Partners section if needed.

Each pending card shows: applicant name, partner type selected, referral code assigned, who referred them, and when they applied. Accounts cannot log in until approved.

5 · Payouts

Go to Payouts in the sidebar. Two tabs: Pending and All.

Step 1 — Generate Payouts for a Month

1
Select the month
Use the month picker (e.g. 2026-03 for March 2026).
2
Click "Generate Payouts"
The system scans every purchase made that month by students linked to each partner, calculates commission, and creates a payout record. A summary appears showing how many payouts were created and the total amounts.
✓ Commission calculation is automatic

Revenue × partner's commission rate = commission amount. If a partner had two different rates during the month, the system applies each rate proportionally to the days it was active.

Step 2 — Mark as Paid

After you transfer money to a partner (UPI, bank transfer), click Mark Paid on their payout row. Enter the actual amount paid and an optional note (e.g. UTR number). The record is timestamped and locked. Partners can see this update immediately on their own portal.

6 · Partner Toolkit

A file library that partners can browse and download. The admin controls all content.

  • Upload — give the file a title, pick a category (Poster, Template, WhatsApp, Webinar, Other), and upload the file
  • Delete — remove any file that's outdated or no longer needed
  • Partners see it — whatever you upload appears on partners' Toolkit page automatically

Good things to upload: promotional posters, WhatsApp message templates, social media creatives, referral guide PDFs, course brochures.

7 · Network Tree View

Go to Network in the sidebar. This shows the entire partner hierarchy as an expandable tree — who is under whom at every level.

  • Colour-coded by status: ● Active ● Pending
  • Badge by type: Campus Ambassador Education Associate Institutional Partner
  • Each node shows: name, referral code, total students, total revenue, total clicks, count of all descendants
  • Click the arrow to expand/collapse. Top 2 levels are auto-expanded.
  • Use the search box to instantly find any partner by name, code, or type — the tree auto-expands to show matches
  • Hover any row and click the external link icon to jump to that partner's full detail page

How a Partner Joins

The self-registration flow — no admin involvement until approval

Any existing partner can recruit new partners by sharing their join link. This is the self-service onboarding flow.

meritlaunchers.com/join/PARTNERCODE

Step-by-Step Registration Flow

1
Applicant opens the link
They see a branded form showing who referred them (the partner's name/code). The referral code is pre-filled automatically.
2
They fill in their details
Full Name (required) · Phone (10-digit, required) · Email · City · Partner Type (Campus Ambassador / Education Associate / Institutional Partner) · Password (they set their own)
3
They submit
Account is created in pending status. A confirmation screen appears: "Application submitted! You will receive login credentials once approved."
4
The referring partner approves
The application appears in the referring partner's Network page under "Pending Applications". They click Review & Approve and confirm. The account is instantly activated. The new partner can log in with the email and password they chose.
5
Inform the new partner
Out-of-band: the referring partner (or admin) tells them to log in at meritlaunchers.com/partner/login with the email and password they set.
⚠️ Cannot log in until approved

If a self-registered applicant tries to log in before approval, they see a clear error: "Account pending approval." There is no auto-approval.

ℹ️ Admin can also monitor all pending applications

The Marketing Admin's Pending page shows the full queue across the entire network. While the referring partner does the approval, the admin can see everything and manually intervene if needed.

Referral Tracking — How It Works

What is tracked, what isn't, and why

What We Track: Clicks

Every time someone clicks a referral link (/ref/CODE or /ref/CODE/channel), we record:

Data RecordedDetail
Partner codeWhich partner's link was clicked
ChannelWhatsApp, Instagram, YouTube, Direct, Seminar, etc.
DateThe calendar date of the click
Anonymised IPHashed — for deduplication only. Not stored as raw IP.
🔁 Deduplication — 1 click per person per day

If the same person clicks the same partner's link on the same channel on the same day, only 1 click is counted. This prevents click inflation. A second click the next day counts as a new click.

Channel-Specific Links

Partners can use different link variants to track which platform is sending them traffic:

PlatformLink Format
WhatsAppmeritlaunchers.com/ref/CODE/wa
Instagrammeritlaunchers.com/ref/CODE/ig
YouTubemeritlaunchers.com/ref/CODE/yt
Seminar/Offlinemeritlaunchers.com/ref/CODE/seminar
Direct (default)meritlaunchers.com/ref/CODE

What Happens After a Click

1
Click is logged
The server records the click with partner code, channel, date, anonymised IP.
2
User is redirected to Play Store
The student is immediately sent to the Merit Launchers app on the Google Play Store. The referral code is appended as a Play Store referrer parameter.
3
Student downloads the app and signs up
When they create an account in the app, if they entered a referral code (or it was carried from the Play Store referrer), their account is permanently linked to that partner.
⚠️ Important: What we cannot track automatically

The Merit Launchers app is built on a no-code platform (Robin). We do not have access to the app's source code. This means:

  • We cannot automatically detect when a referred student installs the app
  • We cannot read in-app payment events in real-time
  • Attribution works when students manually enter their referral code when signing up, or when it's carried from the Play Store install referrer

Bottom line: Clicks are tracked 100% automatically. Student and purchase attribution depends on the student providing the referral code during signup in the app.

Student Attribution — How a Student Gets Linked to a Partner

When a student creates an account in the app and enters a referral code, that code is sent to our system and stored permanently on the student's account. All their future purchases are attributed to that partner.

This is permanent — once attributed, the student stays with that partner even if they click another link later.

Key Rules & Business Logic

Important behaviours the marketing team should know

Commission is on purchases only

Free signups generate zero commission. Only completed course purchases count toward a partner's commission.

Payouts are manual, monthly

The admin generates payouts once a month. Money does not go automatically — the admin reviews amounts and manually marks each as Paid after transferring.

Attribution is permanent

Once a student is linked to a partner via their referral code, they stay with that partner forever. There is no reassignment.

Rate changes are not retroactive

Changing a partner's commission rate does not recalculate old payouts. Only future payout generations use the new rate.

Referral codes are unique & permanent

Once assigned, a code cannot be reused by anyone else. All historical data ties to that code forever.

Click deduplication is per-day

The same person clicking the same partner's link on the same day counts as 1 click. A click the next day is a new click.

Pending partners cannot log in

Self-registered applicants have no access until their referring partner (or the admin) explicitly approves them.

Sub-partner visibility is limited

A partner sees their direct sub-partners' performance only. They cannot see two levels down. They see identity only (not financial data) about their upline.

The Complete Flow — How Everything Connects

Marketing Admin │ ├── Creates partner directly → shares credentials → partner logs in │ └── Monitors pending applications from the entire network Active Partner │ ├── Shares /ref/CODE → Student clicks │ ↓ │ Click logged (channel, date, anon IP) │ ↓ │ Student redirected to Play Store │ ↓ │ Student downloads app & enters referral code │ ↓ │ Student permanently attributed to partner │ ↓ │ Student buys a course │ ↓ │ Revenue added to partner's account │ ↓ │ End of month → Admin generates payout │ ↓ │ Admin marks as Paid → Partner sees it instantly │ └── Shares /join/CODE → Prospective partner fills form ↓ Pending in partner's Network page ↓ Partner clicks Approve & Activate ↓ New partner account active — logs in immediately ↓ (same cycle repeats)
